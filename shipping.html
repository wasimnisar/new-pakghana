Shipping & Delivery

Shipping Times

Shipping time may vary depending on the destination and product availability. For most international deliveries, the typical shipping time is between 5 to 15 business days.

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated based on the size, weight, and destination of your order. We always work with trusted logistics partners to offer you the most competitive shipping rates. Final shipping costs will be shared with you at the time of order confirmation.

Delivery Options

  • Standard Shipping - Cost-effective option with longer delivery time.
  • Express Shipping - Faster delivery for urgent orders.
  • Sea Freight - Best for large or bulk shipments, slower delivery time.
  • Air Freight - Ideal for faster bulk delivery.

Order Tracking

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a tracking number to follow your shipment online.
